QUESTION: Assuming friction is negligible, how long will it take an object to stop if it has an initial velocity of 5 meters per second, if the average braking force is 20 Newtons, and if the objects mass is 20 kilograms? 
ANSWER:   5 SECONDS (Solution: v = at, f = ma => t = v/a = v m / f = 20 x 5 / 20 = 5 seconds)
